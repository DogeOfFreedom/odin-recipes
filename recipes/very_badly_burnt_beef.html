Burnt Beef

Description

Ayo, da bef bunt. Wah do sire?

I take no responsibility for bodily injuries caused

Ingredients

  • Bef
  • Olive Oil
  • Nitroglycerine

Steps

  1. Add some olive oil to a pan set to low heat
  2. Add your beef and let it fry for 2 minutes
  3. Throw some Nitroglycerine on it and watch as your whole house becomes the pan and you, the steak.
  4. If you made it this far, how are you alive?
